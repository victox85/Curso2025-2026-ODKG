Analysis of Selected Datasets

R1: Smart cities domain

All selected datasets come from Cary Open Data. They describe smart-city transportation and mobility: pedestrian/bicycle/cars counters and EV charging stations. These clearly belong to the smart cities domain.

R2: CSV format

Each dataset is available as CSV and has been downloaded in that format.

R3: Open license

All datasets are published under the CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain) license. This allows redistribution and publication.

R4: Linkable entities

The datasets contain real-world locations (sensor sites, charging stations with latitude/longitude) and the town of Cary, NC. These can be linked to external datasets such as DBpedia or GeoNames.

R5: Documentation (optional)

The Cary Open Data portal provides descriptions and metadata for each dataset, including schema and notes about collection methods.

R6: Multiple sources (optional)

Two datasets are used: Eco-Counter traffic data and EV charging data. They cover different aspects of smart mobility in the Cary town.
