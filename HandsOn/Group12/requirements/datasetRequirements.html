Dataset Requirements

Main dataset: ELADESTELLEOGD — Public EV charging stations in Vienna (Open Government Data, data.gv.at).
Portal URL: data.gv.at (dataset page)

Complementary pool: Transport datasets from data.gv.at (verkehr wien).
Portal search: data.gv.at search

Short description

ELADESTELLEOGD provides the locations and attributes of public e-charging points (e.g., operator, address, geographic coordinates, connector types, nominal power, opening/parking info. Fields vary by release). This enables smart-city applications around smart energy & mobility management (EV infrastructure planning, accessibility, capacity mapping).

From the same portal, we will select at least one transport CSV (e.g., traffic count locations, parking facilities/zones, etc) to enrich our application and demonstrate multi-source linking.

R1–R6 Evaluation

ELADESTELLEOGD (main dataset)

RequirementStatusEvidence / Rationale
R1. Smart city domain Meets EV charging infrastructure is core to smart mobility & energy (electromobility, decarbonization, infrastructure planning).
R2. CSV availability Meets The dataset includes downloadable files (CSV is provided).
R3. Open license Meets Published under Austria’s OGD terms on data.gv.at
R4. Linkable to generic entities Meets Records include lat/lon, address, and station identifiers → linkable to districts (Bezirke), census grids, road network, and external Knowledge Graphs (e.g., Wikidata place items).
R5. Documentation (optional) Meets Portal page provides metadata and field descriptions.
R6. Multiple sources (optional) Meets (enrichable) We will combine with at least one transport dataset (see below).
