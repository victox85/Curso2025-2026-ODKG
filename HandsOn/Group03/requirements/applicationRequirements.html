Madrid Smart Mobility

What Users Can Do

The application allows residents, cyclists, pedestrians, and urban planners to explore historical mobility and safety data in Madrid, helping them make informed decisions.

  • Identify Safety Hotspots: Analyze areas with frequent accidents or congestion to understand risk zones and plan safer routes.
  • Filter by Area and Time: Focus on specific neighborhoods, dates, or periods to examine mobility patterns relevant to your interests.
  • Plan Safer and Efficient Routes: Combine historical mobility and infrastructure data to make better commuting decisions.
  • Visualize Movement Patterns: Use heatmaps to see where bicycles and pedestrians were most active in the past.
  • Explore Mobility Trends: View bicycle traffic, pedestrian flows, and traffic light locations across different districts using historical datasets.

Additional Notes

  • Accessible on desktop devices.
  • Efficient map and graph rendering even with large datasets.
  • Based on open datasets for transparency and reproducibility.
