Bus App Description

Overview

This application is designed to facilitate the search and location of bus stops, as well as provide estimated arrival times in Madrid. It uses open data to deliver accessible and useful information for users.

Key Features

1. Nearby Bus Information: Users can enter their current location or explore the map to visualize the nearest bus stops with their corresponding numbers.

2. Detailed Information: By selecting a bus stop, users will see an estimated arrival time for the next buses on the hour selected.

Interface

Users can search for bus stops based on their location, bus number, and other features. A map allows them to easily find bus stops near them.
