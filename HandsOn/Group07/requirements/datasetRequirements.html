Dataset Requirements

This document discusses whether each selected dataset satisfies the requirements. R1-R4 must be satisfied. R5-R6 are optional and their degree of satisfaction is discussed. Identifies the licenses of all the selected datasets and those entities in the selected datasets that can be linked with entities in other datasets.

2) Data & Linking Requirements

Linking Attributes (from the Spanish Universities CSVs)

  • Universidad / RUCT_Universidad
  • Centro/Facultad / RUCT_Centro
  • Titulación (Grado) / Codigo_Titulacion
  • Rama_de_conocimiento
  • ECTS_totales / Curso_academico
  • Campus / Municipio / Provincia / CCAA
  • Direccion_postal
  • Latitud, Longitud
  • Plazas_ofertadas
  • Nota_corte_Junio / Nota_corte_Septiembre
  • Idioma_imparticion / Modalidad (presencial/online)
  • URL_plan_estudios
