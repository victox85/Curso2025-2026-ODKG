Functional Requirements

  • Municipal Cultural Centers
  • R1: the dataset contains information about municipal cultural centers in Madrid (addresses, coordinates, service, schedules)
  • R2: The data is available in CSV (also in other formats: JSON, XML, RDF)
  • R3: The dataset is licensed under the open license (CC-BY 4.0) - https://datos.madrid.es/egob/catalogo/aviso-legal
  • R4: Each cultural center includes geographical coordinates, address, and district, making it directly linkable to other real-world entities like neighborhoods, transport stops, or demographic data
  • R5: Documentation is available
  • R6: The dataset can be combined with other datasets also available in the Madrid City Council portal

  • Municipal cultural and leisure activities in the next 100 days.
  • R1: this dataset describes a list of cultural and leisures activities scheduled to take place in the next 100 days and currently taking place in municipal centers such as youth centers, libraries, museums, district councils and senior centers among others.
  • R2: The data is presented in a CSV file as well as other formats such as JSON, XML and RDF.
  • R3: The dataset is licensed under the open license (CC-BY 4.0) - https://datos.madrid.es/egob/catalogo/aviso-legal
  • R4: Each cultural activity is described with the background of the event, where it takes place, date, district, neighbourhood, coordinates and postal code, which makes it suitable to be linked with generic-real world entities.
  • R5:Documentation is available
  • R6:The dataset can be combined with other datasets also available in the Madrid City Council portal
